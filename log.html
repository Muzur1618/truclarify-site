Why this exists

Marcus uploaded his first Suno track to Spotify in January 2025. By March it had 40,000 streams. By April he received a copyright dispute notice — and discovered he had no documentation of his creative process, no PRO registration, and no answer to the one question that mattered: did he actually own this music?

The US Copyright Office requires human creative input for copyright protection. Without a record of yours, you have nothing to stand on. The Creative Decision Log gives you that record — timestamped, structured, and ready if you ever need it.
